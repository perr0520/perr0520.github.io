HTML Tags I Have learned

Tags

h1 (Heading 1)
h1, is a heading. It is used for the most important heading for your page.
p (Paragraph)
p, is used for the start of a paragraph. It has the capability of holding lots of text and will add a space before and after the paragraph.
q (Quote)
q, adds small quotations on the desired sentence or word.
Strong
Strong, adds boldness to any character and helps visually impared people understand strong words that need to be read differently then the rest.
img (Image)
img, has the ability to add any image to your page as long as you know the source, and size the photo correctly.
a (Anchor)
a, is an anchor tag that allows you to add links to you webpage that can open in a new tab or over the current tab if the correct attribute is added along with it.
